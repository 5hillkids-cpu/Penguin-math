## Module
Industrial HVAC Electrical and Control Troubleshooting

## Observation Purpose
Use this checklist to verify that the technician can trace control voltage, interpret safety strings, and diagnose HVAC no-start faults using Georgia Mechanical standards.

2. What evidence ruled out the motor as the first failure?
3. Why is a BAS call not enough to prove the unit should be running?
