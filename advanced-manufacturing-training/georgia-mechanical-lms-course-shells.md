# Georgia Mechanical LMS Course Shells

## Program Shell

### Course Title
Georgia Mechanical Technical Troubleshooting Academy

### Short Description
Scenario-based industrial troubleshooting training for maintenance and technical support teams covering conveyors, HVAC systems, and chillers.

### Long Description
Georgia Mechanical Technical Troubleshooting Academy is a multi-module training series designed to improve troubleshooting performance across controlled industrial systems. Learners practice evidence-based diagnosis using sequence-of-operation analysis, electrical and controls reasoning, instrumentation checks, and restart verification. The series includes instructor-led lessons, virtual labs, and digital learning modules.

### Enrollment Audience
- Maintenance technicians
- HVAC technicians
- Mechatronics apprentices
- Operations support personnel
- Technical leads and supervisors

### Delivery Format
- Blended learning
- Instructor-led classroom delivery
- Rise or Storyline digital reinforcement
- Workbook and scenario lab activities

### Completion Requirements
- Complete all assigned module lessons
- Complete virtual lab activities
- Pass final exam or supervisor signoff assessment

## Module Shell 1

### Course Title
Georgia Mechanical: Conveyor System Electrical and PLC Troubleshooting

### Catalog Blurb
Diagnose conveyor no-start and no-restart faults using PLC evidence, permissive logic, and field-device verification. This Georgia Mechanical course teaches technicians to isolate faults without guesswork and confirm safe restart.

### Learning Outcomes
- Explain conveyor start sequence
- Identify blocked permissives
- Interpret PLC input and output state
- Diagnose no-restart scenarios safely

### Estimated Duration
4.5 hours instructor-led plus 20 to 25 minutes digital reinforcement

## Module Shell 2

### Course Title
Georgia Mechanical: Industrial HVAC Electrical and Control Troubleshooting

### Catalog Blurb
Trace HVAC control voltage, safety strings, and command logic to diagnose no-fan-start faults without unnecessary part replacement. This Georgia Mechanical course builds a repeatable method for separating demand, permission, and final command.

### Learning Outcomes
- Explain the HVAC fan-start sequence
- Trace low-voltage control paths
- Identify blocking safety devices
- Verify safe corrective action and restart

### Estimated Duration
5 hours instructor-led plus 20 to 25 minutes digital reinforcement

## Module Shell 3

### Course Title
Georgia Mechanical: Industrial Chiller Control and Diagnostics

### Catalog Blurb
Interpret controller alarms, compare sensor inputs to field measurements, and diagnose chiller lockouts without defeating protection logic. This Georgia Mechanical course teaches technicians to distinguish real process risk from false instrumentation signals.

### Learning Outcomes
- Explain chiller enable logic
- Interpret lockouts and sensor evidence
- Compare controller truth to field truth
- Diagnose protected-equipment faults safely

### Estimated Duration
5.5 hours instructor-led plus 20 to 25 minutes digital reinforcement
