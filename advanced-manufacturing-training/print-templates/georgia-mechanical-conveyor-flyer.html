Georgia Mechanical Technical Troubleshooting Academy

Conveyor System Electrical and PLC Troubleshooting

Troubleshoot conveyor no-start and no-restart faults using PLC logic, permissive analysis, field-device verification, and safe restart practices.

Who Should Attend

  • Maintenance technicians
  • Mechatronics apprentices
  • Line support technicians
  • Operations support staff

What Learners Will Do

  • Explain the conveyor start sequence
  • Identify blocked permissives and interlocks
  • Interpret PLC input and output evidence
  • Verify corrective action before restart

Training Format

  • Instructor-led technical session
  • Workbook-based guided practice
  • Virtual troubleshooting lab
  • Digital reinforcement module
Georgia Mechanical standard: confirm the symptom, review the sequence, identify the missing condition, verify with data, correct the cause, and verify the restart.
