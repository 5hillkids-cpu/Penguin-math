Industrial Chiller Control and Diagnostics

Diagnose chiller lockouts and protected-equipment faults by comparing controller logic, alarm history, sensor inputs, and field measurements.

Who Should Attend

  • HVAC controls technicians
  • Maintenance technicians
  • Utilities operators
  • Technical leads and supervisors

What Learners Will Do

  • Explain chiller enable and protection logic
  • Interpret alarm history and controller data
  • Compare controller truth to process truth
  • Verify safe reset and restart procedures

Training Format

  • Instructor-led technical session
  • Scenario-based workbook practice
  • Virtual lab exercise
  • Digital reinforcement module
