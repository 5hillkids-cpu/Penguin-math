# Georgia Mechanical Supervisor Coaching Forms

## Purpose
Use these coaching forms when a technician does not meet signoff standards on the first attempt. The goal is targeted improvement, not generic retraining.

## Coaching Workflow
1. Identify the exact performance gap.
2. Tie the gap to a specific troubleshooting behavior.
3. Assign focused practice.
4. Re-observe on a realistic fault scenario.
5. Document improvement or need for escalation.
