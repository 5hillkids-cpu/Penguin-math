# Georgia Mechanical Course Flyer

## Course Title
Georgia Mechanical: Industrial Chiller Control and Diagnostics

## Course Snapshot
Diagnose chiller lockouts and protected-equipment faults by comparing controller logic, alarm history, sensor inputs, and field measurements.

## Who Should Attend
- HVAC controls technicians
- Maintenance technicians
- Utilities operators
- Technical leads and supervisors

## What Learners Will Be Able To Do
- Explain chiller enable and protection logic
- Interpret alarm history and controller data
- Compare controller truth to process truth
- Diagnose false sensor-driven lockouts
- Verify safe reset and restart procedures

## Training Format
- Instructor-led technical session
- Scenario-based workbook practice
- Virtual lab exercise
- Rise or Storyline digital reinforcement

## Estimated Duration
5.5 hours instructor-led plus digital reinforcement

## Why Georgia Mechanical Uses This Course
This course helps technicians protect high-value equipment, avoid repeated resets, and diagnose whether a chiller lockout reflects a real process condition or false instrumentation input.

## Key Topic Areas
- Enable logic and protection states
- Alarm interpretation
- Sensor plausibility checks
- Lockout diagnosis
- Safe restart verification

## Takeaway
Technicians leave with a Georgia Mechanical standard for protected-equipment troubleshooting based on evidence, not assumption.
