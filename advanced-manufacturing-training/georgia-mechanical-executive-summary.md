# Georgia Mechanical Executive Summary

## Program Title
Georgia Mechanical Technical Troubleshooting Academy

## Purpose
This training package equips Georgia Mechanical technicians with a repeatable, evidence-based troubleshooting method for controlled industrial systems. The program focuses on reducing guesswork, improving first-time fix quality, protecting equipment, and strengthening supervisor confidence in technician decision-making.

## Training Scope
The current package includes three branded modules:

1. Conveyor System Electrical and PLC Troubleshooting
2. Industrial HVAC Electrical and Control Troubleshooting
3. Industrial Chiller Control and Diagnostics

Each module includes instructor materials, learner workbooks, digital authoring scripts, virtual lab exercises, assessments, facilitator delivery tools, supervisor observation tools, and rollout documentation.

## Business Value
- Reduces unnecessary part replacement caused by weak diagnosis
- Improves troubleshooting consistency across technicians and supervisors
- Supports safer restart decisions on controlled and protected equipment
- Creates a reusable internal training standard for onboarding and upskilling
- Provides a structured path from instruction to supervisor signoff

## What Has Been Delivered
- Full module packages for all three technical topics
- Rise production scripts and Storyline scene scripts
- Georgia Mechanical branded slide scripts and facilitator guides
- LMS shell copy, certificates, and branded course flyers
- Capstone assessment, final exam, supervisor signoff, and coaching forms
- Print-ready HTML flyers, certificate template, and coaching forms
- Training portal page and handoff-package structure for deployment

## Recommended Rollout
1. Assign digital prework and module enrollment through the LMS.
2. Deliver instructor-led module sessions over a three-week sequence.
3. Use the capstone and final exam to verify understanding.
4. Use supervisor observation checklists and coaching forms for field follow-up.
5. Track completion and signoff through the Technical Troubleshooting Academy.

## Recommended Audience
- Maintenance technicians
- HVAC technicians
- Mechatronics apprentices
- Operations support staff
- Technical leads and supervisors

## Leadership View
This package is ready to support internal training delivery, branded rollout, and supervisor-led reinforcement. The remaining work, if desired, is primarily publishing and presentation polish rather than curriculum development.
